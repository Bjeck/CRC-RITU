Improvements to the Remote Irregular Telegraph

These improvements are provided by the Connective Rail Company (CRC) in collaboration with the Irregular Operations Line Service (IOLS) for use under the Remote Freight Operation Act.

Improvement Listing January 27th 8725, 4:00:00

  1. New added command: /brief. Allows direct communication to your CRC Manager. Refer to the Manual, Section Archives.
  2. Several dates were corroded in the stored newspaper and encyclopedia entries. These have been corrected. For evidence, see Encyclopedia entries on Patrice Ankaram or S. Duquesne, yet others may be affected.
  3. Fixed issue that caused error on reading Introduction_1 after order completion.
  4. Fixed error when quering districts with higher than existing precinct numbers (this now correctly tells you the entry doesn’t exist)
  5. Updated corroded data in encyclopedia entry “Choir Train” — referencing Meryem Grace by a wrong name, etc.
  6. Corrected an issue causing the beginning of certain archive messages to not show properly.
  7. Added seven new Encyclopedia entries. These include (but are not limited to) an entry on: Ismenda Ekoku, Devani Chandrasena and The Hundred Valleys.

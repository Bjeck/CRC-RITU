Welcome to the Remote Irregular Telegraph Unit

By accepting this invitation you will become an Operator in the RIT Unit, the newest initiative from the Connective Rail Company.

You will manage freight on one of our existing Choir Trains, travelling across the Known World, yet while you remain entirely remote.
This is made possible through the Irregular Telegraph, which you will communicate through using a Discord Server.
